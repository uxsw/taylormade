Taylor Made

More and more companies, following the difﬁcult years of 2020-2021, launched websites to engage their audience, and are now looking to enhance the performance of their website, increase trafﬁc volumes and improve conversion. Through TaylorMade, I offer consultancy and training sessions to your website teams, teaching new skills, enhancing existing knowledge and giving conﬁdence in the direction of your website marketing strategy.

The tools available for your website are constantly increasing in complexity, but there are a solid few that an SME can't do without understanding. It's easy to get left behind. With many years of professional experience using tools such as Google AdWords and Analytics, at Taylor Made my goal is to share this knowledge and give you the skills to take control of your website and maximise its performance.

There are lots of training courses and seminars covering these skills, but most are generic and don't always cover what you really need to know. You walk away having no idea where to start. At Taylor Made I offer one-to-one or small group training, so you always learn about what is most relevant and useful to your own website.

Contact me for a better idea of what I can help you with or visit the pricing page for a cost outline.

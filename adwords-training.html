Get the most from your AdWords account

All courses are organised to cover the areas most useful to your organisation. This might include:

  • Advice on your current AdWords set up and how to move ahead
  • Optimising your account along with Google Analytics
  • Tips to improve your ROI
  • Training with other paid advertising tools (Bing, Facebook, LinkedIn)
  • Keyword research, assessing the competition, optimising ad-text and landing page relevancy
  • Getting started with Google Shopping

AdWords can be a valuable way of increasing traffic to your site and boosting sales. If not managed carefully however, it can be costly and confusing.

On a typical AdWords training course, we will review your current AdWords account and identify places where improvement can be made. We explain the cause of any issues in the account and guide you on how to spot and avoid them for the future.

There are several best-practice suggestions we can make for running your Google AdWords account. Top performing accounts are closely monitored and refined through a linked Google Analytics account, to make the most of the datat Google offers you. For this reason, our AdWords training courses are ideally paired with a Google Analytics course.

I have managed AdWord campaigns, monitored Analytics and optimised SEO for startups and international organisations. I understand the expectations and limitations businesses hit when running their websites.

I firmly believe everyone can get a lot more value out of training when it's focussed on their own projects. That's why I'm wary of generic training sessions regarding the managment of your website marketing. We talk, I get to grips with your current level of knowledge, available budget and resources and then put together a training session that's going to give you the most value.

Training might be a few hours or spread over a few days. It's up to you, your budget and how far you want to take your team.

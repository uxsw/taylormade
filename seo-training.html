Website SEO

All courses are tailored to your website.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is critical in getting your website found for your most relevant content.

Optimise your website for SEO

We can train you in specific areas or review your site and provide training in areas we identify as needing the most attention. These might include:

  • A review of your current SEO levels
  • How to use Google Analytics & AdWords to drive your SEO
  • Best practice for simple improvements
  • Recommendations for your ongoing SEO performance

I have run AdWord campaigns, monitored Analytics and optimised SEO for modest startups and international organisations. I understand the expectations and limitations businesses hit when running their websites.

I believe everyone can get more value out of training when it's focussed on their own projects. That's why I don't believe in generic training sessions on managing your website marketing. We talk, we workout your current level of knowledge, available budget and resource availability and then put together a training session that's going to give you the most value.

Training might be a few hours or spread over a few days. It's up to you, your budget and how far you want to take things.
